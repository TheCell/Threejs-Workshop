# Camera
We already used a camera in our first example. In this scene we don't want to move around we want to take a 360° look around our center. There is already a premade camera that can do this. Let's add an `OrbitCamera`. See the [Docs](https://threejs.org/docs/?q=orbit#OrbitControls)

Once we added the camera you should see this scene.
